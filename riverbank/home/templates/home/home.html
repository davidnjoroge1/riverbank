Welcome to Riverbanks SDA Church

At Riverbanks Seventh-day Adventist Church, we are a vibrant community dedicated to living out the teachings of Jesus Christ through faith, service, and fellowship. Rooted in the Adventist tradition, we embrace a holistic approach to spiritual growth, emphasizing the importance of physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

Our mission is to share the love of Christ with our neighbors and beyond, offering support and guidance in every aspect of life. Through worship, prayer, and community outreach, we aim to foster an environment where everyone can experience the transformative power of God's grace.

Join us as we strive to build a welcoming community where faith and service intersect, and where every individual can grow in their relationship with God and with one another.

We prioritize a balanced approach to health, integrating physical, mental, and spiritual wellness. Our goal is to support every aspect of your well-being through comprehensive care.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join Riverbanks SDA Church?

Joining Riverbanks SDA Church is easy! You can attend one of our worship services to get to know us better. For membership inquiries, please visit our "Join Us" page or contact us through our online form. We welcome newcomers and would be delighted to help you become part of our community.

How can I contribute to the church's mission?

You can support Riverbanks SDA Church through tithes and offerings. Visit our "Giving" page for online donation options. We also welcome contributions in person during our services. For alternative donation methods or more information, please reach out to us via email or phone.

What activities and programs do you offer?

Riverbanks SDA Church offers a variety of programs and activities, including Bible study groups, prayer meetings, youth programs, and community outreach events. For detailed information on our schedule and programs, please visit our "Events" page or contact us for personalized information.

How can I get involved with church ministries?

Getting involved is a great way to contribute to our community. You can join one of our ministries by expressing your interest during a service or contacting us through our website. We have various ministries, including outreach, music, and education, and we would love to have you participate.

How do I contact the church office?

You can reach the church office through our contact form on the website. Alternatively, you can email us or call us using the contact information provided on our "Contact Us" page. We are here to assist you with any inquiries or concerns you may have.

Can I attend multiple church events in one week?

Absolutely! We encourage you to participate in as many church events as you like. Simply check our "Events" page for the schedule and register or RSVP as needed. If you need any help with planning or have specific needs, feel free to contact us.
